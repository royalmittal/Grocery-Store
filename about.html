Our History

In the land of rich cultural heritage and traditions, our journey began with a vision - a vision to bring the essence of Indian flavors and quality products to every home. It all started decades ago when our founder, Shri Kumar Mittal, embarked on a mission to provide authentic and wholesome food items to the people of our nation. With a deep-rooted belief in the principles of purity, sustainability, and ethical practices, our humble establishment grew steadily, earning the trust and loyalty of countless customers across the country. Through our unwavering commitment to sourcing the finest ingredients and employing traditional production methods, we have become synonymous with uncompromising quality and impeccable taste. Today, as we stand tall as a leading name in the food industry, we continue to carry forward our legacy of excellence. Our extensive range of products, carefully curated from the diverse corners of our nation, reflects the vibrant tapestry of Indian cuisine. From the fragrant spices of Kerala to the aromatic teas of Darjeeling, every product tells a story and carries the essence of our rich heritage. At the heart of our success lies our unwavering commitment to our core values - integrity, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We take immense pride in being a part of the Indian culinary tradition and strive to deliver products that not only tantalize your taste buds but also nourish your body and soul. As we embark on this journey of flavors and traditions, we invite you to join us in celebrating the diverse culinary treasures of our incredible nation. Experience the magic of India through our products and let us take you on a gastronomic adventure like no other.
